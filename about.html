I’m a classically trained Toronto photographer with nearly twenty years behind the camera. I’ve worked across sports, events, studio setups — pretty much every environment you can point a lens at — and I still genuinely love the craft. There’s something about being behind the camera that never gets old for me.

These days, my work leans clean, crisp, and a little playful. I’m drawn to good lighting, simple compositions, and the small details that give an image its personality. I like shoots that feel relaxed and collaborative — the kind where we can experiment, laugh a bit, and let the natural moments show up on their own.

If you’re into sharp, expressive photos with a laid‑back, curious energy behind the camera, hit me up and let’s make some art.
